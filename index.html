02 — What I've Built

Selected Projects

🤝

Swift · iOS

GoVolunteer

Helps users discover volunteering opportunities matched to their interests via a short quiz, explore recommended organisations to volunteer with, and record their volunteering experiences through an in-app journal — making it easy to give back and track impact.

🐷

Swift · iOS · Published

PibbleBank

A children's savings app that teaches them to set goals, track money, and track their progress as they save up for desired items — building planning and prioritisation skills independently.

🧠

Python · Machine Learning · Hackathon

BigBacks

A BuildingBloCS hackathon submission using ML to recommend food options based on a user's allergies and medical conditions. Trained a classification model with PyCaret on customer health data, using Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib.

📍

Swift · iOS

snRelocate

A Social Studies project prototype addressing the challenge of students locating lost items across multiple school lost-and-found spots. Students can upload found items and browse reports to reunite owners with their belongings.
